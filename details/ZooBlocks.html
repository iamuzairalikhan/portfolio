Zoo Blocks

Dive into Zoo Match 3, a fun and addictive puzzle game where you match adorable animals like kitties, puppies, pigs, and sheep! Enjoy vibrant levels, exciting power-ups, and unique match-3 challenges. Swap, connect, and crush tiles to rescue animals and create powerful combos in this free, engaging adventure!
