Zoo Blocks

Rescue Girl: Solve the Puzzle is a logic-driven pin-pull puzzle game where players must think carefully before every move. By pulling pins in the correct sequence, players use environmental elements like lava, poisonous gas, and traps to defeat monsters, collect treasure, and rescue the girl from dangerous situations. Each level challenges players to plan ahead, as a single mistake can lead to failure.
